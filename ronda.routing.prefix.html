ronda.routing.prefix

prefix
(prefix descriptor prefix)
Create a new RouteDescriptor representing the routes of the given one
prefixed by a bidi-style route, e.g.

    (prefix d "/a")
    (prefix d ["/" :locale])
    (prefix d ["/" [#"de|fr|us" :locale]])
